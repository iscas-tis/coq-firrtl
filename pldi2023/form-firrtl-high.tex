In this section, we describe the main ideas used in the formalization of HiFIRRTL.

A structure called circuit diagram is used in the formalization.

\begin{definition}
A \emph{circuit diagram} $D$ is a triple $(SS, CE, CO)$, where $SS$ is the statement sequence, i.e. a sequence of statements, $CE$ is a component environment, and CO is the connection mapping. 
\end{definition}
